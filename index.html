Key Observations
Bihar & Jharkhand have high coverage (74–78%) but remain among the poorest health performers — indicating implementation quality gaps beyond enrollment.
Tamil Nadu & Kerala have below-average enrollment, partly due to their own state health schemes (CMCHIS, Karunya) which serve similar populations.
Uttar Pradesh needs simultaneous improvement in coverage quality AND health infrastructure — coverage alone hasn't improved outcomes.
